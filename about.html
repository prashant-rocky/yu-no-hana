Our Story

Founded in Tokyo, Yu no Hana was inspired by Japan’s timeless hot springs. Our mission is simple: to bring the therapeutic, calming, and restorative powers of onsen into every home. From handcrafted bath salts to wellness accessories, every product is designed to honor tradition and nurture well-being.

Our inspiration comes from Japan’s most cherished onsen towns — Hakone, Kusatsu, and Beppu. Each of these towns has a rich history of healing waters, cultural rituals, and community bonding.

At Yu no Hana, we emphasize the cultural and emotional value of onsens — wellness, family connection, and tradition. Our products are carefully curated to bring these experiences to elders, families, and busy professionals, helping everyone reconnect with nature and themselves.
